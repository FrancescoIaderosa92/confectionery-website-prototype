About Coco's Chocolatiers

Coco's Chocolatiers has a rich history. The company was started back in 1925 during the time when Capone owned this city. Coco's Chocolatiers sttruggled to stay afloat because Capone demanded his protection money.

After Coco's Chocolatiers was able to sell enough of its chocolates and save up a good amount of money, Coco's Chocolatiers decided to uproot from Chicago and relocate to Canada in 1953.

Our company now proudly makes its famous chocolates out of a small shop downtown inside the Byward Market. Coco's Chocolatiers has now proudly stood in this location for the past 65 years.
